Regular Expressions are useful for Pattern Matching, which can be used for Validation purpose

[a-z] - Lowercase Characters
[A-Z] - Uppercase Characters
[0-9] - All Numbers
[./,] - Special Characters
\s - All Whitespaces
\S - All Non-Whitespaces
\w - [a-z] + [A-Z] + [0-9]
\W - Other than these combination [a-z] + [A-Z] + [0-9] (Spaces)
\d - [0-9]
\D - All characters other than [0-9]
. - Anything [a-z], [A-Z], ., space, [0-9]
^ - Starts with
$ - Ends with
? - zero or 1
+ - one or more
* - zero or more

Flags:
g - global
i - case insensitive, both uppercase and lowercase pattern will be matched
m - multiline
